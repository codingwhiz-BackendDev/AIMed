If any of the following side effects occur, the pre-
scribing physician should be notified as soon as possible:
• breathing problems, coughing or wheezing
• irregular, fast, or pounding heartbeat
• slow heartbeat (less than 50 beats per minute)
• skin rash
• swollen ankles, feet, or lower legs
Other side effects may occur. Anyone who has
unusual symptoms after taking calcium blockers should
contact the prescribing physician.
