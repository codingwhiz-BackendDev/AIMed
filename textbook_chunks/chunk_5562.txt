Fallopian tubes—The thin tubes that connect the
ovary to the uterus. Ova (eggs) travel from the
ovary to the uterus. If the egg has been fertilized, it
can implant in the uterus.
Fertilization—The joining of the sperm and the
egg; conception.
Implantation—The process in which the fertilized
egg embeds itself in the wall of the uterus.
Ovulation—The release of an egg (ovum) from the
ovary.
