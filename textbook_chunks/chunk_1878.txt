People with certain medical conditions or who are
taking certain other medicines can have problems if they
take cephalosporins. Before taking these drugs, be sure
to let the physician know about any of these conditions:
ALLERGIES. Severe allergic reactions to this medicine
may occur. Anyone who is allergic to cephalosporins of
any kind should not take other cephalosporins. Anyone
who is allergic to penicillin should check with a physician
before taking any cephalosporin. The physician should
also be told about any allergies to foods, dyes, preserva-
tives, or other substances.
