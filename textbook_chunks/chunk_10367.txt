The symptoms of encephalitis range from very mild
to very severe and may include:
• headache
• fever
• lethargy (sleepiness, decreased alertness, and fatigue)
• malaise
• nausea and vomiting
• visual disturbances
• tremor
• decreased consciousness (drowsiness, confusion, delir-
ium, and unconsciousness)
• stiff neck
• seizures
