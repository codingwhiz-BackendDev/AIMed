the condition causing it. An acute infection such as pneu-
monia may require antibiotics, an asthma-induced
cough may be treated with the use of bronchodialators,
or an antihistamine may be administered in the case of an
allergy. Physicians prefer not to suppress a productive
cough, since it aids the body in clearing respiratory sys-
