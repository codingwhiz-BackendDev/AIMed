and constant blood analysis is essential before, during,
and after treatments. Surgical candidates should undergo
extensive pre-operative evaluation with imaging studies,
blood chemistry analysis, stabilized health status, and
readiness of staff for any potential complications and cell
biopsy analysis. Patients with other pre-existing chronic
disease may require intensive post-operative monitoring.
