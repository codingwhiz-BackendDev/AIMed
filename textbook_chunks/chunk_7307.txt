Eschar—A hardened black crust of dead tissue
that may form over a wound.
Pressure ulcer—Also known as a decubitus ulcer,
pressure ulcers are open wounds that form when-
ever prolonged pressure is applied to skin covering
bony outcrops of the body. Patients who are
bedridden are at risk of developing pressure ulcers.
Pressure ulcers are commonly known as bedsores.
Sepsis—A severe systemic infection in which bac-
teria have entered the blood stream.
