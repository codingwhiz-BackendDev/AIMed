People with certain medical conditions or who are
taking certain other medicines may develop problems if
they also take calcium channel blockers. Before taking
these drugs, the prescribing physician should be
informed about any of these conditions:
ALLERGIES. Anyone who has had a previous unusual
reaction to any calcium channel blocker should let his or
her physician know before taking the drugs again. The
physician should also be notified about any allergies to
foods, dyes, preservatives, or other substances.

blockers during pregnancy have not been studied in
