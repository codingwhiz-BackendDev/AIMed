Chemotherapy is administered in different ways,
depending on the drugs to be given and the type of can-
cer. Doctors decide the dose of chemotherapy drugs con-
sidering many factors, among them being the patient’s
height and weight.
Chemotherapy may be given by one or more of the
following methods:
• orally
• by injection
• through a catheter or port
• topically
