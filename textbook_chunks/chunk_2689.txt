Empyema—Pus in the pleural cavity.
Hemothorax—Blood in the pleural cavity.
Pleural cavity—The area of the chest that includes
the lining of the chest cavity, the space the lungs
are located in, and the membrane covering of the
lungs.
Spontaneous pneumothorax—Air in the chest
cavity that occurs because of disease or other nat-
urally occurring cause. Air and blood together in
this space is called a pneumohemothorax.
