An eye examination is performed by an ophthalmol-
ogist M.D. or D.O. (doctor of osteopathy), or an optom-
etrist (O.D.) to determine if there are any pre-existing or
potential vision problems. Eye exams may also reveal the
presence of many non-eye diseases. Many systemic dis-
eases can affect the eyes, and since the blood vessels on
the retina are observed during the exam, certain problems
may be uncovered (e.g., high blood pressure or diabetes).
