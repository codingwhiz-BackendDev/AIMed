Axon—Skinny, wire-like extension of nerve cells.
Myelin—A fatty sheath surrounding nerves in the
peripheral nervous system, which help them con-
duct impulses more quickly.
Nerve conduction testing—Procedure that mea-
sures the speed at which impulses move through
the nerves.
Neuropathy—A condition caused by nerve dam-
age. Major symptoms include weakness, numb-
ness, paralysis, or pain in the affected area.
Peripheral nerves—Nerves throughout the body
that carry information to and from the spinal cord.
