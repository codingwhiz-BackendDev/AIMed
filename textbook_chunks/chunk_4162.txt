A defect that develops in the fetus in which there is a
narrowing of the aortic arch, the main blood artery that
delivers blood from the left ventricle of the heart to the rest
of the body. Coarctation of the aorta is diagnosed in both
newborns and adults. Approximately 10% of newborns with
congenital heart disease have coarctation of the aorta.
