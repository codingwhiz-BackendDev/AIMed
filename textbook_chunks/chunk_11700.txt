Aerobic—Exercise training that is geared to pro-
vide a sufficient cardiovascular overload to stimu-
late increases in cardiac output.
Calisthenics—Exercise involving free movement
without the aid of equipment.
Endurance—The time limit of a person’s ability to
maintain either a specific force or power involving
muscular contractions.
Osteoporosis—A disorder characterized by loss of
calcium in the bone, leading to thinning of the
bones. It occurs frequently in postmenopausal
women.
