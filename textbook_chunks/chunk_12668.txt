For 72 hours prior to collecting samples, patients
should avoid red meats, NSAIDs (including aspirin),
antacids, steroids, iron supplements, and vitamin C,
including citrus fruits and other foods containing large
amounts of vitamin C. Foods like uncooked broccoli,
uncooked turnips, cauliflower, uncooked cantaloupe,
uncooked radish and horseradish and parsnips should be
avoided and not eaten during the 72 hours prior to the
examination. Fish, chicken, pork, fruits (other than mel-
ons) and many cooked vegetables are permitted in the diet.
