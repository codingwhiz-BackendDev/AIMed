Treatment varies depending on the specific type of
Chlamydia causing the infection. A newborn with
Chlamydia trachomatis improves rapidly with ery-
thromycin. Chlamydia psittaci infection is treated with
tetracycline, bed rest, oxygen supplementation, and
codeine-containing cough preparations. Chlamydia
pneumoniae infection is treated with erythromycin.
