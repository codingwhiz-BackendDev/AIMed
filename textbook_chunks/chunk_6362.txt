During infancy many patients with cri du chat do not gain
weight or grow normally. Approximately 30% of infants
with cri du chat have a congenital heart defect. Hypotonia
(poor muscle tone) is also common, leading to problems
with eating and slow, but normal, development. Mental
retardation is present in all patients with cri du chat, but
the degree of mental retardation varies between patients.
