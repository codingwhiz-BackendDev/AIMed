Ganser’s syndrome—An unusual factitious disor-
der characterized by dissociative symptoms and
absurd answers to direct questions.
Malingering—Pretending to be sick in order to be
relieved of an unwanted duty or obtain some other
obvious benefit.
Munchausen by proxy—A factitious disorder in
children produced by a parent or other caregiver.
Munchausen syndrome—A factitious disorder in
which the patient’s symptoms are dramatized and
exaggerated.
