After about five days, most patients can leave the
hospital. They can resume all normal activities, including
work, after four to six weeks of recuperation at home.
In properly evaluated patients, there is a very good
chance that disk removal will be successful in easing
pain. Even in patients over age 60, disk surgery has a
“good to excellent” result for 87% of patients. Disk
surgery can relieve both back and leg pain, but the great-
est pain relief will occur with the leg pain.
