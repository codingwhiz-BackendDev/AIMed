Foreskin—A covering fold of skin over the tip of
the penis.
Glans—The cone-shaped tip of the penis.
Hernia—Bulging of abdominal structures through
an abnormal opening in the muscular wall.
Hydrocele—Collection of fluid in the scrotum.
Hypospadias—A congenital deformity of the penis
where the urinary tract opening is not at the tip of
the glans.
Phimosis—A tightening of the foreskin that may
close the opening of the penis.
Prepuce—A fold like the foreskin that covers the
clitoris; another name for foreskin.
