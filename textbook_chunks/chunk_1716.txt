The characteristic symptoms of orbital cellulitis are
eye pain, redness, swelling, warmth, and tenderness. The
eye may bulge out and it may be difficult or impossible
to move. Temporary loss of vision, pus drainage from the
eye, chills, fever, headaches, vomiting, and a general ill
feeling may occur.
