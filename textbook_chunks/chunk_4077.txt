Migraine headache—An intense throbbing pain
that occurs on one or both sides of the head. The
headache is usually accompanied by other symp-
toms, such as nausea, vomiting, and aversion to
light.
Prophylactic—Referring to treatment that prevents
symptoms from occurring.
Tension-type headache—A dull pain that seems to
exert pressure on the head; the most common
form of headache.
