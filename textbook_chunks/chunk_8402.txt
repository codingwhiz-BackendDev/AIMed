People who are moderately to severely overweight
can derive substantial health benefits from a weight-loss
diet. A weight reduction of just 10–20 (4.5–9.1 kg) can
result in reduced cholesterol levels and lower blood pres-
sure. Weight-related health problems include heart dis-
ease, diabetes, high blood pressure, and high levels of
blood sugar and cholesterol.
