• Core biopsy. A procedure wherein a larger piece of tis-

• Incisional biopsy. A surgical procedure wherein a piece
of the lump is removed through an cut (incision).
• Excisional biopsy. A surgical procedure wherein the
entire lump is removed through an cut (incision).
