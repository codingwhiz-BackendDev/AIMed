Supplements that are used to reduce inflammation
have been used to treat costochondritis. Examples of
such supplements include ginger root, evening primrose
oil, bromelain, vitamin E, omega-3 oils, and white wil-
low bark. Glucosamine/chondroitin sulfate, which may
aid in the healing of cartilage, has also been used. Other
alternative therapies include acupuncture and massages.
