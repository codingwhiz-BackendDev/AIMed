Atrial fibrillation—A condition in which the
upper chamber of the heart quivers instead of
pumping in an organized way.
Atrial flutter—A rapid pulsation of the upper
chamber of the heart that interferes with normal
function.
Ventricular fibrillation—A condition in which the
lower chamber of the heart quivers instead of
pumping in an organized way.
Ventricular tachycardia—A rapid heart beat, usu-
ally over 100 beats per minute.
