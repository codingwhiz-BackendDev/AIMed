Approximately 85% of people do well with conserv-
ative CMP treatment. The remainder still have severe
pain and may require arthroscopic surgery to repair the
tissues inside the knee joint. In more severe cases, open
surgery may be required to realign the kneecap and per-
haps other corrections.
