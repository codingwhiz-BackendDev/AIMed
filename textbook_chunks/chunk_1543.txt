Contact lenses and cataract glasses (aphakic lenses) are

Antibiotic drops to prevent infection and steroids to
reduce inflammation are prescribed after surgery. An eye
shield or glasses during the day will protect the eye from
injury while it heals. During the night, an eye shield is
worn. The patient returns to the doctor the day after
surgery for assessment, with several follow-up visits over
the next two months to monitor the healing process.
