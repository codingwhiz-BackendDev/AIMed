HYPERNATREMIA. Sodium helps the kidneys to regu-
late the amount of water the body retains or excretes.
Consequently, individuals with elevated serum sodium
levels also suffer from a loss of fluids, or dehydration.
Hypernatremia can be caused by inadequate water
intake, excessive fluid loss (i.e., diabetes insipidus, kid-
ney disease, severe burns, and prolonged vomiting or
diarrhea), or sodium retention (caused by excessive sodi-
