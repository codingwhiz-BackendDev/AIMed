Fluoroquinolones are antibiotics, medicines used to
treat infections caused by microorganisms. Physicians
prescribe these drugs for bacterial infections in many
parts of the body. For example, they are used to treat
bone and joint infections, skin infections, urinary tract
infections, inflammation of the prostate, serious ear
infections, bronchitis, pneumonia, tuberculosis, some
sexually transmitted diseases (STD), and some infec-
tions that affect people with AIDS.
