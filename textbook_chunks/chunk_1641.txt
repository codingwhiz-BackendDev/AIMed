Fluoroscope—A specialized x-ray machine used
to visualize the placement of the catheter when
attempting to correct irregular heartbeats.
Pacemaker—An electrical device that has elec-
trodes attached to the heart to electrically stimu-
late the heart to beat normally. Pacemakers can be
internal (placed under the skin) or external, with
the electrodes placed on the skin or threaded
through a tube placed into the heart.
