Cardiac arrest—Temporary or permanent cessa-
tion of the heartbeat.
Cardiopulmonary—Relating to the heart and the
lungs.
Defibrillation—A procedure to stop the type of
irregular heart beat called ventricular fibrillation,
usually by using electric shock.
Resuscitation—Bringing a person back to life after
an apparent death or in cases of impending death.
Ventricular fibrillation—An irregular heartbeat
where the heart beats very fast but ineffectively. Ven-
tricular fibrillation is fatal if not quickly corrected.
