Detoxification therapists use a variety of healing
techniques after a diagnosis is made. The first step is to
eliminate a patient’s exposure to all toxic or allergenic
substances. These include heavy metals, chemicals, radi-
ation (from x rays, power lines, cell phones, computer
screens, and microwaves), smog, polluted water, foods,
drugs, caffeine, alcohol, perfume, excess noise, and
stress. If mercury poisoning has been determined, the
patient will be advised to have mercury fillings from the
teeth removed, preferably by a holistic dentist.

the body’s detoxification process. Dietary change is
immediately enacted, eliminating allergic and unhealthy
