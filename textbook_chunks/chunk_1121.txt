Carboxyhemoglobin (COHb)—Hemoglobin that
is bound to carbon monoxide instead of oxygen.
Hemoglobin (Hb)—A molecule that normally
binds to oxygen in order to carry it to our cells,
where it is required for life.
Hypothermia—Development of a subnormal body
temperature.
pH—A measurement of the acidity or alkalinity of
a fluid. A neutral fluid, neither acid nor alkali, has
a pH of 7.
