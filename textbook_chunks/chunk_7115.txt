Cystoscopy is a diagnostic procedure which is used to view
the bladder, collect urine samples, and examine the prostate
gland.This procedure also enables biopsies to be taken.
The primary instrument used in cystoscopy is the cysto-
scope, a tube which is inserted through the penis into the
urethra, and ultimately into the bladder. (Illustration by Elec-
