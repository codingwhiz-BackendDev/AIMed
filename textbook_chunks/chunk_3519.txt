Cholinergic drugs should be avoided when the
patient has any sort of obstruction in the urinary or diges-
tive tracts, such a a tumor, or severe inflammation which
is causing blockage.
They should be used with caution in patients with
asthma, epilepsy, slow heart beat, hyperthyroidism, or
gastric ulcers.
