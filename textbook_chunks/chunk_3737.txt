Early, aggressive treatment of all infections is criti-
cal to the successful management of CGD. Patients are
treated with antibiotics and immune serum. Antibiotics
are used at the first sign of infection. Immune serum is a
source of antibodies that help fight infections. Interferon
gamma is an experimental treatment for CGD that has
shown promising results. There is no cure for the under-
lying cause of chronic granulomatous disease
