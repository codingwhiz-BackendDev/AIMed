Use corticosteroids with caution in patients with the
following conditions:
• osteoporosis or any other bone disease
• current or past tuberculosis
• glaucoma or cataracts
• infections of any type (virus, bacteria, fungus, amoeba)
• sores in the nose or recent nose surgery (if using nasal
spray forms of corticosteroids)
• underactive or overactive thyroid
• liver disease
• stomach or intestine problems
• diabetes
• heart disease
• high blood pressure
• high cholesterol
• kidney disease or kidney stones
• myasthenia gravis
• systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE)
• emotional problems
• skin conditions that cause the skin to be thinner to
bruise more easily
