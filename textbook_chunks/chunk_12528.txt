Physically, fatigue is characterized by a profound
lack of energy, feelings of muscle weakness, and slowed
movements or central nervous system reactions. Fatigue
can also trigger serious mental exhaustion. Persistent
fatigue can cause a lack of mental clarity (or feeling of
mental “fuzziness”), difficulty concentrating, and in
some cases, memory loss.
