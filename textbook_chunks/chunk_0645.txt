At recommended doses, caffeine can cause restless-
ness, irritability, nervousness, shakiness, headache, light-
headedness, sleeplessness, nausea, vomiting, and upset
stomach. At higher than recommended doses, caffeine can
cause excitement, agitation, anxiety, confusion, a sensa-
tion of light flashing before the eyes, unusual sensitivity to
touch, unusual sensitivity of other senses, ringing in the
ears, frequent urination, muscle twitches or tremors, heart
arrhythmias, rapid heartbeat, flushing, and convulsions.
