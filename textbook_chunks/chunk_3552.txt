Physical therapy offers treatments that may help
CMP patients. Aqua therapy has the benefit of exercising
the knee without putting stress on it and it also strength-
ens the thigh muscles. Biofeedback can be used to learn
tensing and relaxing specific muscles to relieve pain.
These techniques have the benefit of no side effects.
Massage therapy might be beneficial as well. Calcium,
minerals, and vitamins as part of a balanced diet will
aid healing and help prevent further problems.
