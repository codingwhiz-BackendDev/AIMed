Cortisol is a potent hormone known as a glucocorti-
coid that affects the metabolism of carbohydrates, pro-
teins, and fats, but especially glucose. Cortisol increases
blood sugar levels by stimulating the release of glucose
from glucose stores in cells. It also acts to inhibit insulin,
thus affecting glucose transport into cells.
