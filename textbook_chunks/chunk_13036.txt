• Mammogram. An x-ray examination of the breast.
• Ultrasound scan. A technique that uses sound waves to
display a two-dimensional image of the breast, showing
whether a lump is solid or fluid-filled (cystic).
• Fine-needle aspiration biopsy. A minor procedure
wherein fluid or cells are drawn out of the lump through
a small needle (aspirated).
