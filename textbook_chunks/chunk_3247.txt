Cholecystitis—Infection and inflammation of the
gallbladder, causing severe pain and rigidity in the
upper right abdomen.
Cholelithiasis—Also known as gallstones, these
hard masses are formed in the gallbladder or pas-
sages, and can cause severe upper right abdomi-
nal pain radiating to the right shoulder, as a result
of blocked bile flow.
Gallbladder—A hollow pear-shaped sac on the
under surface of the right lobe of the liver. Bile
comes to it from the liver, and passes from it to the
intestine to aid in digestion.
