Careful attention to symptoms can help determine
the underlying cause of the dizziness. Underlying prob-
lems may be benign and easily treated or they may be
dangerous and in need of intensive therapy. Not all cases
of dizziness can be linked to a specific cause. More than
one type of dizziness can be experienced at the same
time and symptoms may be mixed. Episodes of dizziness
may last for a few seconds or for days. The length of an
episode is related to the underlying cause.
