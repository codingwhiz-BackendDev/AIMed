There are more than a dozen viruses that can cause
encephalitis, spread by either human-to human contact or
by animal bites. Encephalitis may occur with several
common viral infections of childhood. Viruses and viral
diseases that may cause encephalitis include:
• chickenpox
• measles
• mumps
• Epstein-Barr virus (EBV)
